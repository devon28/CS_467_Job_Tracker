Job Tracker

Instructions

This job tracking app allows you to keep track of jobs, skills, and contacts.

As you apply for jobs or gain new skills:

  • Add any jobs you apply for to the Jobs page
  • Include relevant skills and contacts for each job
  • Add new skills you gain to the Skills page
  • Add new contacts to the Contacts page

This allows you to see which skills are needed for different jobs and identify in-demand skills.

Use this app to organize your job search and highlight your most relevant skills!
